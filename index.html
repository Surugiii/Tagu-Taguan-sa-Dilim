About

Tagu-taguan sa Dilim is a dark survival horror game created by our team, where you explore an abandoned house, search for clues, and outsmart a creature that learns your every move.

Story

Tagu-taguan sa Dilim is a survival horror game where you play as a person trapped inside a dark, abandoned house. Your goal is to find the real key and escape before a mysterious creature finds you.


You only have a flashlight to help you see. The key is hidden in a random room, and sometimes you‚Äôll find fake keys made by the creature to trick you. While exploring, you can find paper clues left by missing kids that can help you understand how to escape and what the creature is afraid of.


The game has a scary and dark 3D look with real sounds. You can hide under beds, inside closets, and you‚Äôll need to listen carefully to know where the creature is. The monster gets smarter the longer you play, so you need to keep changing how you move.

Team

We are PlayTure, a team of third-year BSIT students from Datamex College of St. Adeline Valenzuela. Our team is excited to present our latest game concept, Tagu-taguan sa Dilim. We are dedicated to creating engaging and visually compelling games that resonate with players.

Inspired by the Filipino game tagu-taguan, our project aims to provide a fun yet intense experience. We are confident that this game will captivate audiences and enhance the HypeHype gaming portfolio.
